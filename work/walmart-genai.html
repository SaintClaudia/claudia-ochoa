Overview

From exploratory ask to Creative Director

This didn't start as a project. After a meeting with Paradox — a leader in Chat-to-Apply — leadership asked what a Walmart-specific version could look like. Instead of recreating what we'd seen, I proposed something differentiated.

Within 24 hours I delivered an end-to-end concept: apply, schedule, accept, and learn about Walmart entirely through one AI-driven conversation. The concept reshaped internal thinking about AI-enabled hiring and earned early leadership support — positioning me as Creative Director for the initiative.

The Problem

Millions of candidates. A process designed to lose them.

Before redesigning anything, I mapped the full candidate journey — from initial job search through third-party systems, assessments, and offer acceptance. What I found was a fragmented experience that actively drove people away.

The Solution · Ask Sam

A hiring assistant built for first-time job seekers

Sam ("Sparky And Me") was designed to align with the Me@ product family. The visual language, tone, and every interaction were intentional — warm, direct, and built to make a 17-year-old applying for their first job feel capable and seen.

A distinct visual language was created from scratch — not a reskinned third-party tool — to feel unmistakably Walmart while setting a new bar for conversational hiring design.

01
Returning candidate recognition
Sam remembers context and surfaces relevant jobs immediately — no re-entry required.
02
Availability in one step
Shift selection replaces the 80-screen assessment. Three taps to match.
03
Selection state
Morning selected — custom illustrated icons with Walmart-native color system activate on tap.
Design Decisions

Four principles that shaped every screen

01
Zero account wall
Candidates can start a conversation before creating an account. Commitment follows value, not the other way around.
02
Human tone, always
Every Sam message was written to sound like a helpful person, not a system. No jargon, no passive voice, no corporate hedging.
03
One system, full journey
Apply, schedule, and accept — all within Sam. No redirects, no re-entering information, no wondering if you're in the right place.
04
Distinct visual language
Built from scratch to feel unmistakably Walmart — custom icons, brand-native type, and interaction patterns designed for a first-time applicant.
Outcome

A concept that became a platform

The 24-hour concept earned leadership buy-in and repositioned the project from exploratory to Walmart's new candidate application experience. Deloitte is currently integrating the direction into the broader hiring platform.

As Creative Director, I held the vision through delivery — ensuring the final experience stayed true to the human, conversational principles established in the original concept.
